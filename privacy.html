Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-22

Top Of The Tok helps users scan public TikTok creator profile pages and rank public posts by save-to-like ratio.

This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok.

Information collected

When a user chooses to scan and publish a creator, the extension stores public TikTok profile and post metrics in the shared Top Library database, including:

  • creator handle, display name, and avatar URL;
  • post ID, caption, post URL, cover image URL, and post date where available;
  • views, likes, saves, comments, shares, calculated ratios, and scan/upload timestamps.

Information not collected

The extension does not collect TikTok passwords, TikTok cookies, private messages, payment details, personal contact details, or browsing history outside TikTok creator/profile pages used with the extension.

How information is used

Published scans are used to display creator ratio dashboards in the extension's Top Library. Published scan data is public within the extension for other extension users to view.

Third-party services

The Top Library stores scan data in Supabase. TikTok pages are accessed directly by the user's own browser session.

User control
