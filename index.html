Privacy Policy Support

About the extension

Top Of The Tok lets users scan public TikTok creator profile pages from their own browser session and view public post metrics ranked by save-to-like ratio.

The extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok.

Replace the support contact in privacy.html and support.html before submitting to the Chrome Web Store.
